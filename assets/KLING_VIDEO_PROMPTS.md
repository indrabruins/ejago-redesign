# Ejago Agency — Kling Video Prompt Document

Three video concepts for the Ejago AI marketing agency website. All use the brand color palette: warm cream background (#faf9f6), indigo primary (#6366f1), emerald secondary (#10b981), orange CTA (#f97316).

---

## Video 1: Hero Brand Manifesto Animation

**Duration:** 6 seconds  
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (1920×1080)  
**Style Notes:** Bold, cinematic, high-energy entrance. Text should feel like it's flying in with weight and momentum. Dark-to-light contrast pulse on text reveal. Ethereal background with the indigo/emerald blob morphing subtly.

**Exact Prompt:**

> Cinematic 6-second brand manifesto animation, bold text reading "EJAGO" flying in from left to right with motion blur, text constructed from geometric fragments that assemble mid-flight, indigo (#6366f1) and emerald (#10b981) color scheme, on a warm cream (#faf9f6) background, followed by tagline "AI Marketing That Actually Grows" emerging letter by letter, geometric particles trailing behind each letter, modern premium agency aesthetic, dark indigo glow on text edges, 4K render quality

---

## Video 2: Process Visualization — 4 Phases

**Duration:** 8 seconds  
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (1920×1080)  
**Style Notes:** Clean, sequential, animated infographic style. Each phase icon builds on the previous one. Flowing connecting lines between phases. Subtle pulse/glow on active phase. Modern and professional.

**Exact Prompt:**

> Animated 8-second process visualization showing 4 phases of Ejago agency workflow, phase 1 "Discover" with magnifying glass icon pulsing with indigo light, phase 2 "Strategy" with roadmap icon appearing and connecting via emerald line to phase 1, phase 3 "Build" with construction icon emerging and connecting, phase 4 "Grow" with upward arrow icon launching upward with orange (#f97316) glow, clean timeline connector flowing left to right, warm cream (#faf9f6) background, each phase labeled elegantly in sans-serif font, smooth ease-in-out transitions, modern digital agency infographic style, 4K render quality

---

## Video 3: Results Showcase — Metrics Counter Animation

**Duration:** 5 seconds  
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (1920×1080)  
**Style Notes:** Data-driven, confident, satisfying. Numbers should count up with easing. Chart lines draw themselves in. Subtle particle effects on milestone numbers. Professional dashboard aesthetic with clean typography.

**Exact Prompt:**

> Animated 5-second results showcase, sleek data dashboard visualization, large metric numbers counting up from zero: "340%" in indigo (#6366f1) glowing text, "2.4M" impressions in emerald (#10b981), "180+" clients in orange (#f97316), bar chart bars growing from left to right with smooth spring animation, subtle particle sparkle effects on each number milestone, glowing accent lines connecting data points, floating on warm cream (#faf9f6) background, professional analytics aesthetic, dark gradient overlays on data cards for depth, 4K render quality

---

## Technical Notes for Production

- All videos: export as MP4 H.264, 30fps
- Brand fonts: use Inter or Sora (Google Fonts available in brand)
- Alpha channel available for hero-blob.mp4 overlay use
- Hero animation can loop with 2s hold at end for web embedding
- Process video: each phase auto-aligns to 2s segments for easy editing
- Metrics video: numbers are animated via After Effects expressions for easy value swapping
