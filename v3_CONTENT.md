---

### Case Study 1: BloomBar (Skincare DTC)
**Challenge:** Strong product, decent traffic, conversion rate stuck at 1.2%.
**What we did:** Full site redesign + email flow rebuild (welcome, cart abandon, post-purchase).
**Before:** 1.2% conversion rate | $0 email revenue | No clear brand positioning
**After:** 2.8% conversion rate | $14K/mo email revenue | Consistent brand voice across 12 touchpoints
**Timeline:** 90 days

---

### Case Study 2: RunRight (Running Gear DTC)
**Challenge:** CAC rising 40% YoY while revenue stayed flat. Attribution model broken.
**What we did:** Full marketing audit → cut 2 broken ad channels → rebuilt attribution with UTM-first approach → launched Klaviyo with proper segment logic.
**Before:** $68 CAC | 0-email revenue | 60% ad spend wasted
**After:** $42 CAC | $18K/mo email revenue | 22% revenue growth in 6 months
**Timeline:** 6 months

---

### Case Study 3: Nomad (Footwear DTC)
**Challenge:** New product line launching into saturated market. Needed positioning + launch campaign from scratch.
**What we did:** Brand positioning workshop → landing page → Meta launch campaign with UGC-first creative strategy.
**Before:** $0 product revenue | No brand presence | No launch infrastructure
**After:** $180K product line revenue in 90 days | 4.1% CVR on landing page | 3.2x ROAS on Meta
**Timeline:** 90-day full launch
